## 🎨 Quick Reference

### Aspect Ratios
- `--ar 1:1` - Square (Instagram)
- `--ar 3:4` - Portrait
- `--ar 4:3` - Standard
- `--ar 16:9` - Landscape/Cinema
- `--ar 9:16` - Stories/Reels
- `--ar 2.39:1` - Cinemascope

### Version Tags
- `--v 6` - Latest version
- `--niji` - Anime style

### Quality Tags
- `--q 2` - Higher quality
- `--style raw` - Less stylized
- `--iw 0.5` - Image weight
