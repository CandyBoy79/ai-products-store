# Print-on-Demand Guide - Sell Custom Products

## 🔰 What is Print-on-Demand?

You design. POD company prints & ships. You keep profit!

## 📦 Best Products to Sell

### High Margin
- T-shirts ($15-25 profit)
- Hoodies ($20-35 profit)
- Mugs ($5-15 profit)
- Tote bags ($5-10 profit)

### High Volume
- Stickers
- Posters
- Phone cases

---

## 💰 Pricing Formula

```
Base Price + Your Margin = Selling Price

Example:
T-shirt base: $10
Your margin: $15
Sell for: $25

Profit: $15 per sale
```
