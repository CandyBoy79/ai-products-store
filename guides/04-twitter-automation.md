## 🎯 Viral Formulas

### The Hook
- Start with a number
- Use bold claim
- Ask a question

### The Thread Formula
1. Hook (stop the scroll)
2. Context (why it matters)
3. Points (3-10 insights)
4. CTA (follow, like, reply)

### The Viral Tweet
- Controversial take
- Surprising stat
- Emotional story
- Useful list

## 📈 Growth Hacks

### 1. Engage First
- Reply to big accounts
- Add value to conversations
- Be helpful, not spammy

### 2. Use Hashtags
- 1-3 relevant tags
- Mix popular + niche
- Don't overdo it

### 3. Post at Right Times
- Your audience's timezone
- Test different times
- Use analytics

### 4. Collaborate
- DMs with peers
- Spaces participation
- Guest tweets

## ⚠️ Things to Avoid

- 🚫 Buying followers
- 🚫 Auto-follow/unfollow spam
- 🚫 Posting too often
- 🚫 Being too salesy
- 🚫 Ignoring replies
- 🚫 Copying others
